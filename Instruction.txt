**Text Sentiment Analysis App - Instructions**

Welcome to the Text Sentiment Analysis App! This app allows you to perform sentiment analysis on text, transcribe audio, and more. Follow the instructions below to use the app effectively.

**Using the Text Analysis Feature:**

1. **Enter a News Article URL:**
   - In the sidebar, locate the "Enter the News Article Link" input field.
   - Enter the URL of a news article you want to analyze.
   - Click the "Submit" button.

2. **Upload a File (Optional):**
   - If you have a text document to analyze (DOCX, TXT, or PDF), you can upload it.
   - Click the "Upload file" button and select your document.
   - The app will automatically analyze the uploaded file.

3. **Transcribe Audio (Optional):**
   - If you have an audio file to transcribe, use the "Upload an audio file" section.
   - Supported audio formats include MP3, WAV, and FLAC.
   - Click the "Transcribe" button to transcribe the audio.

4. **Clear All Inputs:**
   - At any point, you can reset all inputs by clicking the "Clear All" button in the sidebar.

5. **View Sentiment Analysis:**
   - Once you've provided a URL or uploaded a document, the app will perform sentiment analysis.
   - The sentiment analysis results will be displayed in the "Sentiment Indicator" tab.
   - You'll see scores for positive, negative, neutral, and overall sentiment.

**Using the Word Cloud Feature:**

1. Go to the "Word Cloud" tab to see a visual representation of the most frequent words in the text.
2. The word cloud helps you quickly identify important keywords.

**Using the Content Viewer Feature:**

1. Visit the "Content" tab to view the entire content of the article or document.
2. You can edit and scroll through the text to explore the content.

**Using the Information Retrieval Feature:**

1. Head to the "Information Retrieval and Relevance Ranking" tab.
2. Enter a query in the "Enter your query" input field.
3. Click the "Search in the text" button.
4. The app will retrieve the most relevant sentences based on your query.

**Part-of-Speech (POS) Tagging:**

1. To perform POS tagging on the content, click the "Perform POS Tagging" button.
2. POS tags for each word in the text will be displayed.

**Additional Information:**

- This app supports various document formats, including DOCX, TXT, and PDF.
- Audio transcription supports formats like MP3, WAV, and FLAC.
- Ensure that you have an active internet connection for web-based operations.
- For assistance, click the "Clear All" button and re-enter your inputs.

That's it! You're all set to use the Text Sentiment Analysis App. If you encounter any issues, please refer to the "README" for more information or contact our support team.

Happy text analysis!
